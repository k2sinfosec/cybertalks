Encouraging Cybersecurity Awareness in the Inland Empire 

It seems like we're always hearing about another company who's data has been breached. If you are a business owner who is unsure how to avoid joining those statistics, come and join us at Cyber Talks.

Our mission is to equip our community with the tools necessary to keep their business secure in this digitial landscape. Each meeting will have a 15-minute practical presentation on an area of security related to keeping customer and business data safe. There will be plenty of open networking time and food.

Whether your business is Real Estate, Legal, Financial, Hospitality, Retail, Manufacturing, Government Contracting, Aftermarket auto, Aerospace, Healthcare, Education, Technology, or anything else, there is something to learn at Cyber Talks.
